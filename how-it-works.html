How FundCambo Works
FundCambo is the best place to fundraise, whether you are an individual, group, or organisation.

Start your fundraiser

  1. Set your fundraiser goal: Decide on the amount you need to raise. Consider the costs your project entails and set a realistic target that would cover all your needs.
  2. Tell your story: Explain why you're raising funds. Be transparent and heartfelt; your story is the soul of your campaign and can inspire others to contribute.
  3. Add a picture or video: Visuals can greatly enhance your message. Include high-quality images or a short video to make a strong, personal connection with potential donors.
  4. Watch a video tutorial: If you're new to fundraising, consider watching our tutorial videos. They can guide you through the process and offer valuable tips.

Share with friends

  1. Send emails: Craft a personalized email message and send it to your contacts. A direct appeal can often lead to early donations to get your campaign going.
  2. Send text messages: A quick text can be an effective nudge. Reach out to close friends and family members who might prefer this more direct form of communication.
  3. Share on social media: Leverage the power of social networks. Post your fundraiser link on your social media accounts to reach a wider audience quickly.
  4. Watch a video tutorial: For strategies on sharing your fundraiser and spreading the word effectively, watch our series of sharing tutorials.

Manage donations

  1. Accept donations: Monitor your fundraising page regularly. Make sure donations are being processed correctly and update your total as needed.
  2. Thank donors: Show appreciation to your supporters. Personal thank-you messages can go a long way in building a supportive community.
  3. Withdraw funds: Follow the steps to safely withdraw the funds you have raised. Ensure you understand the procedures and timelines for transferring funds to your account.
